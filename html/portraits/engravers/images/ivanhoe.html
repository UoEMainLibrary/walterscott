Ivanhoe, engraved by James Heath after Richard Westall (1809)

From: Sir Walter Scott, Ivanhoe: A Romance by the Author of Waverley. London: Hurst, Robinson & Co., 1820.

The engraving depicts a scene from Chapter VI of Ivanhoe, where the hero, disguised as a Palmer, warns Isaac, the Jew of York that his life is in danger.
