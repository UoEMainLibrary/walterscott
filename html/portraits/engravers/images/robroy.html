The Encounter, engraved by Charles Heath after John Massey Wright (1837)

From: The Waverley Keepsake, and Abbotsford Album, or Beauties of Sir Walter Scott. London : R. A. Charlton, 1837 (facing p. 199).

The plate depicts Rob Roy interrupting a duel between Francis Osbaldistone and Rashleigh (Rob Roy, ch. 25).
