Thomas Cranmer, engraved by William Holl the Younger after an unknown artist (1829)

From: The Portrait Gallery of Distinguished Poets (London: Wm. S. Orr & Co., 1853)

This portrait of Protestant theologian Thomas Cranmer was first published in May 1829 in E. Lodge's Portraits of Illustrious Personages. It is derived from a 16th-century woodcut by an unknown artist.
