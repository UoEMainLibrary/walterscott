Sir Walter Scott, photogravure of watercolour by William Nicholson (1902)

Frontispiece to: John Gibson Lockhart, Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott (Boston: Houghton, Mifflin and Co., 1902), vol. II.

The photogravure was made from a watercolour obtained by William Erskine, Lord Kinedder in 1817 (possibly as a gift from Scott).
