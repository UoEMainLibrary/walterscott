Sir Walter Scott in his Study at Castle Street, engraved by Robert Charles Bell after Sir John Watson Gordon

Frontispiece to: Portrait of Sir Walter Scott: And Five Engravings in Illustration of 'The Pirate' ([Edinburgh: Royal Association for the Promotion of the Fine Arts in Scotland], 1871)

Bell's engraving is made from an undated posthumous portrait of Scott painted by Sir John Watson Gordon in the 1840s or 1850s.
