Sir Walter Scott, engraved by Edward Smith after Sir David Wilkie (1829)

Published: Edinburgh: Robert Cadell; London: Moon, Graves, & Boys, 1829.

Edward Smith's engraving of the 1824 portrait by Sir David Wilkie was subsequently published as the frontispiece to vol. I of The Poetical Works of Sir Walter Scott (Edinburgh, Cadell, 1830).
