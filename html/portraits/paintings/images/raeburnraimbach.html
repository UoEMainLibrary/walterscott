Sir Walter Scott, engraved by Abraham Raimbach after Sir Henry Raeburn (1811)

Raimbach's engraving of Raeburn's 1808 portrait of Scott was published by R.H. Cromek & J. Sharpe in 1811. The above print is the frontispiece to vol. I of the Works of Walter Scott, Esq. (Edinburgh: Longman and Co., 1812).
