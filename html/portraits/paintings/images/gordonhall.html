Sir Walter Scott, engraved by Henry Bryan Hall
after Sir John Watson Gordon

From: Sir Walter Scott, The Lay of the Last Minstrel: A Poem (London: Charles Daly, 1837) (front.)

Hall's miniature engraving of the 1830 painting by Sir John Watson Gordon omits the background view of the Eildon Hills and removes Scott's dog Bran from the foreground.
