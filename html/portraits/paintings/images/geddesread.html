Sir Walter Scott, engraved by William Read after Andrew Geddes (1828)

From: Public Characters: Biographical and Characteristic Sketches, with Portraits, of the Most Distinguished Personages of the Present Age (London: Knight and Lacey, 1828)

Read's engraving is after a drawing by Geddes made from his own 1818 portrait of Scott, or possibly after an earlier engraving by F.C. Lewis.
