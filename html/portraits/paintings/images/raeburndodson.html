Sir Walter Scott, engraved by Richard W. Dodson after Sir Henry Raeburn (1838)

Dodson's engraving of Raeburn's 1808 portrait of Scott was published as the frontispiece to the American edition of J.G. Lockhart's Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart., published by Carey, Lea, & Blanchard, Philadelphia, 1838.
