John Morrison and Abbotsford: Bibliography

  • Hogg, James, The Queen's Wake: A Legendary Poem (Edinburgh: G. Goldie, 1813)
  • Hogg, James, The Queen's Wake: A Legendary Poem, 6th edn (Edinburgh: Blackwood, 1819)

  • Scott, Walter, Sir, The Letters of Sir Walter Scott, ed. H.J.C. Grierson (London: Constable, 1932-37)
  • Trotter, Alexander, East Galloway Sketches, or, Biographical, Historical, and Descriptive Notices of Kirkcudbrightshire, Chiefly in the Nineteenth Century (Castle-Douglas, 1901)
