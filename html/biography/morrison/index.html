John Morrison's Plan of the Abbotsford Estate

One of the treasures of Edinburgh University Library's Corson Collection is a manuscript map of Sir Walter Scott's Abbotsford estate drawn for Scott by John Morrison in 1819-20, with the names of fields and plantations added in Scott's own hand (see right).
