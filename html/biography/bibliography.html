Bibliography for Biography Pages

The following works have been used in the construction of the Biography pages of this site and are essential reference tools for anyone researching the life of Scott:

  • Buchan, John, Sir Walter Scott (London: Cassell, 1932)
  • Clark, Arthur Melville, Sir Walter Scott: The Formative Years (Edinburgh: Blackwood, 1969)
  • Corson, James C., Notes and Index to Sir Herbert Grierson's Edition of the Letters of Sir Walter Scott (Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1979)
  • Grierson, H.J.C., Sir Walter Scott, Bart.: A New Life, Supplementary to and Corrective of, Lockhart's Biography (London: Constable, 1938)
  • Johnson, Edgar, Sir Walter Scott: The Great Unknown (London: Hamish Hamilton, 1970)
  • Lockhart, J. G., Memoirs of the Life of Sir Walter Scott, Bart. (Edinburgh: R. Cadell, 1837-38)
  • Millgate, Jane, Walter Scott: The Making of the Novelist (Edinburgh: Edinburgh University Press, 1984)
  • Millgate, Jane, Scott's Last Edition: A Study in Publishing History (Edinburgh: Edinburgh University Press, 1987)
  • Pearson, Hesketh, Walter Scott: His Life and Personality (London: Methuen, 1954)
  • Pope-Hennessy, Una, Dame, Sir Walter Scott (London: Home & Van Thal, 1948)
  • Quayle, Eric, The Ruin of Sir Walter Scott (London: Rupert Hart-Davis, 1968)
  • Scott, Walter, Sir, The Journal of Sir Walter Scott, Bart., ed. W.E.K. Anderson (Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1972)
  • Scott, Walter, Sir, The Letters of Sir Walter Scott, ed. H.J.C. Grierson (London: Constable, 1932-37)
  • Scott, Walter, Sir, New Love Poems: Discovered in the Narrative of an Unknown Love Episode with Jessie --- of Kelso, ed. Davidson Cook (Oxford: Blackwell, 1932)
  • Sutherland, J.A., The Life of Sir Walter Scott: A Critical Biography (Oxford: Blackwell, 1995)
  • Todd, William. B., and Ann Bowden, Sir Walter Scott: A Bibliographical History, 1796-1832 (New Castle, DE: Oak Knoll Press, 1998)
