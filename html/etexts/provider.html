E-Texts by Provider

Listed alphabetically below are all the sites that we have traced which offer Walter Scott e-texts. In each case, we provide a full list of the texts offered. Click on the site name to go to that site's own index. Click on individual titles to go directly to the text.

To search texts by genre (Novels, Short Fiction, Longer Poems, Short Poems, Miscellaneous Prose, Correspondence, Works edited by Scott), go to our Index page.

For those not wishing to browse the entire list, the following sites offer especially strong selections in particular genres:

  • Novels: Munseys (24 novels), Project Gutenberg (23), Arthur's Classic Novels (22), Knowledgerush (21), Bookrags, Literature Network, Making of America Books (17 each).
  • Short Fiction: Knowledgerush, Project Gutenberg (6 texts each), Arthur's Classic Novels, Munseys (5 each).
  • Longer Poems: Oldpoetry (8 poems), Making of America Books (6), Bookrags, Literature Network, Project Gutenberg (4 each), Munseys (3).
  • Shorter Poems: Oldpoetry (96 poems), Making of America Books (46), Bartleby (20).
  • Miscellaneous Prose: English Server (10 texts), Arthur's Classic Novels (8).
