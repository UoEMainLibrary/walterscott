Please note that we cannot offer valuations of editions of Scott's work. As a general point, few volumes published outside Scott's own lifetime (1771-1832) are of significant commercial value. Scott remained a best-selling author throughout the nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, and popular editions of his work were published in great numbers both in Great Britain and the United States. If you feel that your edition of Scott may be of exceptional value, we recommend that you contact a reputable antiquarian or second-hand book-dealer.
