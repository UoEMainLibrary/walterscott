Recent Critical and Biographical Works On Sir Walter Scott: An Annotated Bibliography

Follow the links below to access an annotated bibliography of critical and biographical material wholly or substantially devoted to Sir Walter Scott published from the year 2000 onwards. The Author and Subject Indexes cover both monographs and articles/chapters.

It is eventually hoped to extended coverage back to 1990. For earlier critical and biographical material, consult the following volumes:

  • James Clarkson Corson, A Bibliography of Sir Walter Scott: A Classified and Annotated List of Books and Articles Relating to his Life and Works, 1797-1940 (Edinburgh; London: Oliver and Boyd, 1943)

  • Jill Rubenstein, Sir Walter Scott: A Reference Guide (Boston: G. K. Hall, 1978)

  • Jill Rubenstein, Sir Walter Scott: An Annotated Bibliography of Scholarship and Criticism, 1975-1990 (Aberdeen: Association for Scottish Literary Studies, [1994])

For material published between 1990 and 2000, a good source is the Bibliography of Scotland.
